About

15th Century Norgediantic Arc is a fantasy story about the Norgediantics, beings created with a sacred purpose: to follow the will of the Creator of everything.

The Norgediantics were brought into existence to fulfill the roles entrusted to them. Each of them carries a duty and a place in the greater design of the world. Their journey unfolds in a time filled with conflict, mystery, and the echoes of ancient history.

Genre: Fantasy • Mixed History • War • Mystery

About the Creator

The creator of this manga goes by the name Aylmer, an independent artist from the Philippines.

This manga was created with the hope of inspiring readers. Even in a world that may seem dark, light will eventually come. Often, darkness appears first before the light reveals itself.

Purpose of This Website

This website was created so that readers can enjoy the story of 15th Century Norgediantic Arc freely online.

Future Updates

New chapters will be added in future updates. The story and artwork are currently still in progress, and more content will be released as development continues.
